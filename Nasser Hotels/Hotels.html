Describe the residence

The Kings Arms is located in London, just 500 meters from Oxford Street, and offers express check-in and check-out, non-smoking rooms, a bar, free Wi-Fi, and a terrace. The accommodation is less than 1 km from Piccadilly Theatre, 1 km from Queen's Theatre, and less than 1 km from the British Museum. The property provides a 24-hour front desk, room service, and luggage storage for guests. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge, a kettle, a shower, a hairdryer, and a wardrobe. The rooms in the hotel have a private bathroom with free toiletries. A continental breakfast is available every morning at The Kings Arms. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Carnaby Street, Dominion Theatre, and Prince Edward Theatre. London City Airport is the nearest airport to The Kings Arms, located 13 km from the property
