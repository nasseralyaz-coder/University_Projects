Describe the residence

The 5-star boutique Milestone Hotel Kensington enjoys an ideal location between the Knightsbridge and Kensington districts in central London. It features a swimming pool with counter-current swim technology, a fitness center, and a luxurious spa. This hotel with its unique ambiance overlooks Kensington Palace and Gardens and is just a 10-minute walk away from the verdant Hyde Park and the Royal Albert Hall. Each air-conditioned room is uniquely designed and offers elegant and romantic atmospheres, along with a 24-hour reception desk and modern technology. The luxury rooms come with antique-style furnishings, fresh flowers, and plush fabrics, and include a flat-screen TV with a DVD player, a work desk, a seating area, and a minibar. The en-suite bathroom provides complimentary toiletries and a hairdryer. Room service includes free light snacks such as sweets, homemade cakes, seasonal fruits, or candles. Guests at the unique Milestone Hotel Kensington can enjoy a range of traditional dishes at the on-site Cheneston's Restaurant, where dishes are prepared using local and organic products as much as possible. The chefs cater to individual requests, while Stables Bar offers a special ambiance with a selection of over 400 types of vintage wine. Guests can also enjoy cocktails in the elegant winter garden or have tea by the fire in the lounge. Dating back to the 19th century.
