Describe the residence

This historic and central hotel enjoys a fantastic central location between Piccadilly and Regent Streets and offers spacious and elegant rooms equipped with everything guests need to stay in the city center. The spacious rooms at The Dilly are elegantly furnished and feature private bathrooms equipped with bathrobes and comfortable slippers. Guests can also enjoy free newspapers, as well as a refrigerator and 24-hour room service. The Dilly is just a 10-minute walk from the shops on Oxford Street and Bond Street and less than a 5-minute walk from Piccadilly Square. Leicester Square and Covent Garden can also be reached within a 10-minute walk from the accommodation. The Dilly's health club contains a steam room, and guests can exercise in the fitness center and modern squash courts. The Dilly also features a dance studio available for private hire or dance lessons. The Terrace restaurant at The Dilly has an outdoor terrace that offers views of Piccadilly, and afternoon tea is served with children-friendly menus so that all family members can enjoy it. Guests can experience Indian cuisine flavors at Madhu’s restaurant in the hotel's Oak Room. The Dilly has 7 meeting rooms and event areas. The hotel does not accept contactless smart card payments, so the physical card is required.
